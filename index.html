Studio Policies

Clear expectations for a smooth, elevated appointment experience.

Policies help protect your scheduled time, preserve the studio flow, and ensure every client receives the same attentive level of service.

Arrival

Please arrive on time. Late arrivals may require a shortened service or rescheduling to protect the next client’s appointment.

Cancellations

We recommend at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or appointment changes whenever possible.

Guests

To maintain a calm and comfortable studio environment, additional guests are discouraged unless arranged in advance.

Design Requests

Inspiration photos are welcome. Please send them before your appointment so enough time can be reserved for your desired look.

Suggested Deposit Policy

Professional, client-friendly wording you can keep or customize.

A deposit may be required to reserve your appointment and will be applied toward your service total. Deposits are non-refundable and may be forfeited for no-shows, last-minute cancellations, or repeated rescheduling.

If you are running late, please notify the studio as soon as possible. Accommodation depends on the schedule for the day.

For custom nail art, correction work, or large changes from your booked service, pricing may be adjusted based on time and complexity.
